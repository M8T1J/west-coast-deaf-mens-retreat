Support the West Coast Deaf Men's Retreat

Help Strengthen Deaf Men for Christ

For many Deaf men, opportunities for spiritual growth, fellowship, and biblical teaching in a fully accessible environment are rare. The West Coast Deaf Men's Retreat (WCDMR) provides a place where Deaf men can gather, grow in God's Word, and build strong brotherhood in Jesus Christ.

Lives are changed when men step away from the distractions of life and focus on the Lord together.

Your gift today helps Deaf men encounter Christ, grow in His Word, and be strengthened for a lifetime of faith.
