About WCDMR 2026

The goal of this event is to bring Deaf men in Kingdom of God.

The West Coast Deaf Men's Retreat is a three-day summit of Prayer, worship, and Fellowship, taking place November 6-8, 2026 at Pine Crest Camp in Twin Peaks, California.

Join us for this transformative experience where Deaf men from across the West Coast come together to grow in faith, build community, and strengthen their relationship with God.
