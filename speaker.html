Jesse Macias

Guest Speaker

Jesse has served in Deaf ministry, evangelism, and outreach for over 20 years. He is passionate about helping men grow in spiritual maturity and bold faith.

Ministry Highlights

  • Two decades of Deaf ministry service and leadership
  • Evangelism and testimony outreach across churches
  • Mentorship for Christian men and families
  • Biblical teaching focused on transformed living

Retreat Focus

Jesse's sessions will encourage men to live faithfully, lead with humility, and stand boldly for Christ in daily life.
